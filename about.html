About Blue Dot

Blue dot is a modern company that specializes in borehole drilling and installation and everything related to the trade. We have done a variety of projects since our establishment and we are the best in the business. The Blue Dot technical team is extremely competent and it values high standards.

We ensure long standing partnerships with our customers and we do our best to keep them satisfied. With our continued commitment to development and change, we look forward to the future and all the challenges and opportunities that it will offer.

Blue Dot Irrigation is a borehole and irrigation systems installation company based in Harare and we have an excellent reputation.
